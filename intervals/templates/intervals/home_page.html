Music Interval Training

This skill helps those who are seeking to improve the ability to hear and identify music intervals. Alexa randomly plays intervals for the user to guess and responds whether the response is correct or incorrect. www.musicintervaltraining.com saves this data a provides a platform for the user to view their metrics and play intervals they wish to practice.

{% comment %}

How to use:

To Get Started: Say, "Alexa, open Music Interval Training"

You will guess the type of interval played and Alexa will confirm whether correct or not.

You will be able to view your progress on this site through our dynamic charts

Start by Saying:

"Alexa, open Music Interval Training"
