Music interval ear training for the aspiring musician

An interval is the difference in pitch between two sounds

Music Interval Training

This app will help you master identifying different music interval patterns. You will hear two notes played from Alexa and guess the interval played. This application will keep a score of correct and incorrect answers so that you can see your progress and identify the most challenging intervals.


How to use:

To Get Started: Say, "Alexa, open Music Interval Training"

Alexa will prompt you whether to play a random music interval or not

You will guess the type of interval played and Alexa will confirm whether correct or not.

You will be able to view your progress on this site through our dynamic charts


Start by Saying:

"Alexa, open Music Interval Training"

"Alexa, play me an interval"
